Volunteer Management System (BCA Project)

During my BCA, I wanted to build something useful for my college. I noticed that event volunteering was not well organized and students faced difficulty in registering.

So, I developed a Volunteer Management System where students can book slots for events posted by admin or sub-admin.

There are three roles: User, Admin, and Sub-Admin. Admin is managed by the college, and sub-admins are selected students responsible for posting events.

Students can book slots, and the admin/sub-admin can accept or reject requests. Once processed, students receive an email notification.

Database was managed using HeidiSQL.
